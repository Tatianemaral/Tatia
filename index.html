Who We Are

Precision in Construction

Located in the strategic hub of Mogi das Cruzes, São Paulo, LTMA Engenharia e Design Ltda is a forward-thinking construction firm dedicated to the nonresidential sector. We specialize in creating industrial and commercial spaces that stand the test of time.

Under the leadership of Tatiane Palmeira Do Amaral, our firm bridges the traditional gap between structural execution and architectural design. We believe that industrial buildings should not only be functional but also intelligently designed to maximize efficiency.

From foundational work to the final structural finishes, LTMA is committed to safety, regulatory compliance, and engineering excellence in the São Paulo region.
